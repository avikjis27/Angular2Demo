Quiz instructions

  • Select an answer for every question. Unanswered questions will be scored as incorrect.
  • There are three possible question types:
    1. Multiple Choice: click the radio button to indicate your choice. Currently, only one answer can be selected for a multiple choice question.
    2. True/False: click the radio button to indicate your choice.
    3. Matching Answers: select a match from the pop-up list below each item.
  • If you use a wheel button mouse, take care not to accidently change your answers. Sometimes scrolling the wheel will rotate through the answers in a selection list, when you might have meant simply to scroll farther down in the quiz window.
  • Click on the Submit button at the bottom of the page to have your answers graded.
